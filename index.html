Edible Landscaping

Food Forest Folks specializes in helping people grow food in any available space, big or small. We use permaculture principles and past island experience to create beautiful gardens that will thrive in this unique environment that Big Island offers. Our mission is to help you establish food security that will need less outside resources over time (such as mulch and fertilizer).

Our Services

Tree Care

We offer tree pruning and care for established fruit trees. We can help mitigate pests and disease, as well as teach or provide long-term tree care and maintenance. We can also help you plan for and plant new orchards.

Raised Beds

Rased beds and gardens are crucial for growing food on Big Island. We can build any sized raised bed or herb spiral, using as many resources on hand as possible.

Real Estate Scouting

We can evaluate whether an area is good for growing food, identify possible flood conditions or areas suitable for ponds, and trees to keep. We can use our drone for aerial photos, as well as knowledge and experience of the island (and its weather), to offer guidance as you consider what property to buy.

Consultation

Do you have an area that you've wanted to transform but need some ideas? Are you new to the island and need to learn how to work with a new environment, or just want to learn what's growing in your backyard? Want to get a school gardening program together but need some advice on the specifics for growing crops? We work with private and public clients to offer guidance on how to make your idea into reality. We use our consultations as personal classes to teach you anything and everything you want to know to grow your own food successfully.

We can also help during the construction process to take advantage of the machinery on site to utilize tree waste, such as creating privacy and food producing burms- rather than paying thousands to get it hauled away.

Design

We can help you plan out your food forest to utilize your landscape as efficiently and easily as possible. We can also help you create food bearing hedgerows and trellises to provide privacy from your neighbors. Designing can be as informal as verbally brainstorming with the client, or as professional as a digital design overlaying a drone photo of your property.

Our goal is to help you plan out a beautiful, self-sustaining, food producing paradise that is easy to manage and has its own mulch and fertilizer crops.

Education

We are available to teach classes and workshops for larger groups anywhere on Big Island
