Our Services

We specialize in helping people grow food in any available space- big or small. We use permaculture principles and past island experience to create beautiful gardens that will thrive in this unique environment that Big Island offers.

Our mission is to help you establish food security that will need less outside resources over time (such as mulch and fertilizer). We can build raised bed gardens and herb spirals, create food bearing hedgerows for privacy, help with incorporating animals into the system, or simply offer guidance as you move forward with raw land or DIY projects!

Tree Care

If you already have fruit trees established, we offer tree pruning and can help deal with pests and disease, as well as long term tree care and maintenance.

Consultations

If you have raw land, we offer scouting and consultations on how to work with the landscape. We can help make a plan regarding where to put building and garden pads, identify possible flood conditions or areas suitable for ponds, and trees to keep. We can help you take advatage of the machinery on site to utilize tree waste, such as creating privacy and food producing burms- rather than paying thousands to get it hauled away.

If you live on a drier part of the island, we can help you create an oasis that will eventually require little water, and will help protect you from wildfires.

About Us

Daniel has a B.S. in Ecology with a minor in Forestry and specializes in animal care and behavior, landscape design, lilikoi (passionfruit) trellis, privacy walls, fruit varieties/plant identification, human and animal nutrition, water drainage and flood management.

Together, we are a team greater than the separate sum of our parts. We are both from the Midwest, and were raised working part time on our family's farms. We take pride in our work ethic, reliability, and our ability to help you envision and create your own paradise.

Work With Us

When hired, after an initial consultation, we will work with you to create a planting guide, and advise on the steps needed to move forward (black cinder/soil/mulch delivery, flood management, mulch crops and etc). Big Island is a unique planting/growing environment that, unfortunately, mainland (and even other islands') planting/growing customs don't apply to.

We charge $70/hr for consultations, but dont charge if it goes over an hour for 1 acre lots or smaller. 3+ acre lots that are fully cleared can take closer to 2 hours. Our hourly rate is $70/hr total which includes full access to our tools, and trailer if needed. $80/hr + gas outside the Puna/Hilo/Volcano area.
